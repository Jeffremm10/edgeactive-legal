How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Edge app and go to the Profile tab
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  3. Type DELETE in the confirmation field
  4. Tap Delete My Account â€” your account and data will be removed immediately

Note: You must resolve any pending bets before deleting your account. If you have open sports bets, parlays, or unsettled group wagers, the app will prompt you to wait until they are settled.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

Permanently Deleted

  • Your profile information (username, avatar, settings)
  • Sweat Coins balance and transaction history
  • Betting history (sports bets, parlays, group bets)
  • Group memberships and associated activity
  • Achievement progress and badges
  • EdgePass (Battle Pass) progress
  • Powerup inventory and effects
  • VIP membership status

Retained for Legal & Financial Compliance

  • Purchase transaction records (required for tax and refund processing, retained up to 3 years)
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any remaining Sweat Coins, owned avatars, VIP membership time, and EdgePass progress will be lost. Active subscriptions should be cancelled through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store before requesting deletion.
